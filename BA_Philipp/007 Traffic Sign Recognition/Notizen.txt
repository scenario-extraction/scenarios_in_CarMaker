In CM gibt es einen Traffic Sign Sensor
"The Traffic Sign Sensor delivers the following output signals:
• Total number of detected signs
• Distance to traffic sign position (polar coordinates)
• Distance to traffic sign position in sensor frame or mounted frame
• Traffic sign identification given by road
• User defined sign attribute values (e.g. the value of a speed limit sign)
• Additional external rotation of sensor, expressed in mounted frame
- with rotation sequence ZYX
• Additional external travel of sensor, expressed in mounted frame
• Time stamp of updated sensor signals"
(Users Guide Seite 413, mehr: section 20.7)
